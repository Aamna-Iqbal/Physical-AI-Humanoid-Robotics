# Quickstart: Module 2: The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity) Course Content

This guide provides a quick overview for understanding and contributing to the content of Module 2: The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity) Course.

## Module Overview

Module 2 focuses on teaching AI students about physics-based digital twins for humanoid robotics, utilizing Gazebo for physics simulation and Unity for high-fidelity interaction and environment design. It also covers the simulation of various sensors.

## Content Location

The Markdown files for this module are located within the `docs-site` Docusaurus project at:
